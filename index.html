BSides Asheville

BSides Asheville is an annual Information Security conference located in the mountains of Western North Carolina that strives to bring quality speakers and training to the area. We are extremely excited for this year's event to be held at Mojo Coworking in downtown Asheville. Training will take place the evening of Friday, June 26th. Talks and additional activities taking place on Saturday, June 27th. More details regarding speakers, training, volunteering, and tickets will be posted soon!

Call For Papers

CFP is now open, please submit topics to info@bsidesasheville.com. Please be sure to include a brief summary of your talk and personal bio. CFP will close on April 15th.
